    The Great Game of Farkle

    Farkle is a fast paced dice game of busts or big scores. Score by selecting to keep 1's, 5's, triples, or three pairs. Once you get to 500 points on your turn, you have the option of banking your points. From then on out, whenever it is your turn, you can bank your points. Re-roll non-scoring dice to try and get scoring dice. Be careful, if you roll no scoring dice, you Farkle! Your turn is then over and you do not score any points.
